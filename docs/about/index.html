About Lists of Things with Images

Too frequently I find myself thinking, "I wanna look at all the sturgeons to find the coolest one to use for reference" or similar such whimsical thoughts, only to feel thoroughly disappointed when my internet search of, "List of all sturgeons with images" only hands me lists of very few limited sturgeons with images, or lists of all sturgeons without images. Likewise on the rare occassion that I DO find a nice list of sturgeons with images, I find myself thoroughly delighted!

Eventually I realized that I, as a web developer, am more than capable of supplying the world with lists of things with images (⌒ ▽ ⌒) ☆

Why am I doing this? I don't really get anything out of it. By the time I make my own list, chances are I don't need it anymore. But the thing is, maybe at some point there's someone else out there that happens to need a list of sturgeons with images, and will end up just as disappointed as me at the lack of one. I'm making these lists for them (◡ ‿ ◡ ✿)
